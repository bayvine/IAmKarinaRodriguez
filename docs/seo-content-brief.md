## The Main Strategic Takeaway

Karina's current description is thoughtful and differentiated, but it is too abstract to rank well for high-intent local queries by itself.

Right now, the language leans heavily into:

- leadership and executive development consultant
- student wellness and resilience strategist
- body's innate intelligence
- reconnect with your physical self
- harmony between personal and professional lives

That language may be true, but it does not strongly match what people type into Google.

People are more likely to search for:

- executive coach in Denver
- leadership coach in Denver
- career coach in Denver
- burnout coach for executives
- leadership development consultant Denver
- executive coaching for women leaders
- team coaching and workshops Denver

So the site should not abandon Karina's body-based or resilience-based methodology. It should translate it into clearer search language.

- FAQs
- clear CTAs

Especially for `career coach denver`, high-ranking pages are not generic homepages only. They are dedicated landing pages with:

- city in the title

### 5. They cover adjacent search terms on the same page

Examples from competitors include:

- stress management

This helps them rank for more than one exact phrase.
